Section 2.10 Change the Background

In Microsoft Word, the page background of your document can be changed.

Press ALT. Pressing ALT will move you to the ribbon. Press p p C to open the background dialog box. Then TAB to or use ARROWS to the background color you want. Press ENTER.

When making a greeting card or sign, it might be nice to change the background color. This may not be a good idea when writing a research paper. Always check with your instructor before getting too fancy!

Subsection 2.10.2 Tasks

Follow the steps below to change the background of a document.

  • Open an existing document or create a new document.

  • Change the background color to black. Press ALT, then p p C.

  • TAB to "black" and press ENTER. The text should turn white and the background color will be black.

  • Explore changing the background to other colors.

  • You can change the font color as needed. Press CONTROL + A to select all.

  • Press ALT, then H, F, and finally N to bring up the "font" dialog box. TAB through the dialog box. Make changes as needed. Use ENTER and ARROW KEYS to help make changes.

  • Another handy command is to force a new page in a document. Press CONTROL + ENTER to force a new page.
